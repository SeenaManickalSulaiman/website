HBaker’s isn’t just about food — it’s about memories, love, and connection.

It all began in 2009 as Lucky Cafeteria in the heart of Naif, Deira. A small kitchen serving comfort food that made people smile. Over the years, it became a gathering spot, a familiar corner where everyone was welcome.

Today, we reintroduce ourselves as HBaker’s – Your Daily Treat. A fresh name, an elevated experience, yet rooted in the same heartfelt service and flavors.

Whether it’s Kerala breakfast, juicy burgers, or a nostalgic pazhampori with beef — every bite brings back something familiar.

Welcome to HBaker’s. Let every bite feel like home.
